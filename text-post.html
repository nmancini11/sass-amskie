Wilson Lin Category Tags Category Tags

For the first Hawley Earth Fest, held on April 26th 2014, AMSkier organized the first Hawley Species Parade. Children and adults were invited to dress up as their favorite species to slither, fly, walk, crawl and gallop around Bingham Park. The day turned out to be gorgeous, despite the threat of rain. There was a slight chill, but since most of us were covered head to toe in animal and plant costumes, we didn't really mind!

AMSkier Insurance staff began organizing and setting up around 5:30 am. Costume helpers, general volunteers and members from other wonderful organizations such as Woodloch Resort & The Northeast PA Audubon Society joined us shortly after. Children and families got a kick out of the Woodloch mascots and the wonderful craft provided by the Northeast Audobon Society. Hand drawn colorful native birds were glued onto craft pipe cleaner and affixed to headbands. What were the results of this craft? Everyone learned more about our native birds, especially those flying around our heads!
